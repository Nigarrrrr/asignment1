Qualifications

Diploma in Professional Makeup Artistry

A Diploma in Professional Makeup Artistry is a comprehensive program that covers essential skills, including bridal makeup, editorial and fashion makeup, special effects (SFX), and airbrush techniques. This certification is widely recognized in the beauty industry and helps aspiring makeup artists build a strong foundation in both theoretical knowledge and hands-on application.

Associate Degree in Cosmetology

An Associate Degree in Cosmetology provides an in-depth understanding of skincare, beauty treatments, and advanced makeup application techniques. This degree also includes training in hair styling, skin health, and salon management, making it ideal for those who want to expand their expertise beyond makeup artistry and establish a full-service beauty career.
